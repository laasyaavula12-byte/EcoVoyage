🌸 About Japan

A Country Where Tradition Meets Innovation

Japan is one of the world's most fascinating destinations. From centuries-old temples and peaceful shrines to futuristic cities, Japan perfectly blends history, culture and technology. Every season offers a unique experience—from cherry blossoms in spring to colorful autumn landscapes.

💡 Travel Tips

🚄

Travel by Rail

Use the Japan Rail Pass to explore multiple cities conveniently.

💴

Carry Cash

Many local shops and restaurants still prefer cash payments.

🙇

Respect Etiquette

Follow local customs, speak quietly on trains and remove shoes when required.

📱

Stay Connected

Rent a pocket Wi-Fi or use an eSIM for easy navigation.
